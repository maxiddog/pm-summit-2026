Everything you need to know about PM Summit 2026

What should I bring to the summit?
Bring your laptop with Cursor or your preferred AI-enabled IDE installed, along with the Datadog MCP configured. We'll have hands-on workshops where you'll debug real code. Don't forget your badge (sent via email before the event) and comfortable shoes‚Äîwe have a lot of ground to cover!
Is there a dress code?
The dress code is smart casual. Jeans and a nice shirt work perfectly. You'll also receive exclusive PM Summit swag including a hoodie, so dress comfortably!
Will meals be provided?
Yes! We'll provide breakfast, lunch, and snacks both days. There will also be a networking dinner on the first evening at a nearby venue. Please note any dietary restrictions in your registration.
How do I get my free swag?
Head to the Swag Store tab and complete the debugging challenge! Use your AI assistant (Cursor or Cline) with the Datadog MCP to find and fix 3 intentional bugs in the store. Once fixed, complete your checkout and pick up your merch at the registration desk.
Can I submit questions for speakers ahead of time?
Absolutely! Use the Submit Questions tab to send questions to any of our speakers. We'll compile the most upvoted questions and address them during Q&A sessions. You can also ask questions live during the event.
Will sessions be recorded?
Yes, all main stage sessions will be recorded and made available on our internal learning platform within a week after the summit. Workshop sessions are not recorded to encourage open discussion.
What is the Datadog MCP?
The Datadog MCP (Model Context Protocol) is a tool that connects your AI assistant directly to Datadog's observability platform. It allows you to query logs, traces, metrics, and more using natural language. During the summit, you'll learn how to use it to debug applications in real-time.
